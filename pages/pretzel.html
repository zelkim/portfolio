Pretzel: Porting MLS Course Offerings to Discord.

  • Developed a Discord bot to port De La Salle University's course offerings to the Discord platform.
  • Utilized webscraping to gather data from DLSU's My.LaSalle web application.
  • Established a community by open-sourcing the project, opening the opportunity for students from the university to contribute.

    • Github Repository: https://github.com/zelkim/pretzel

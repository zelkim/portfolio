Pakabuhi.ph: my attempt at a startup

Pakabuhi.ph is a startup project me and my team started in 2021 after winning the Catbalogan City Hackathon 2020.


Pakabuhi.ph is a mobile application that connected individual service providers that do laundry, haircuts, plumbing, cleaning, etc. to consumers via mobile application.


The startup was incubated by Samar State University's Technology Business Incubator, which led to the connection with the Provincial Government, Local PESO Office, DOST, and TESDA to obtain a list of potential individual service providers.


However, the startup fell out due to funding issues, and time constraints due to senior high school.

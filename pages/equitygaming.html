Equity Gaming: A temporary escape from reality.

Equity Gaming is a gaming community for a roleplay server on San Andreas Multiplayer, garnering over 20,000 unique users over the course of 3 years, with 200+ daily active players.



What is "roleplaying", and, GTA: San Andreas???

Roleplaying in this context is simulating real life.

In the game, upon registration, you would start off as a blank slate. And the more you play, the more you interact with other people, the more you interact with the environment, the more your character develops. Everything you do in the game will be consequential to what your character becomes.

In the game, you can take on different roles. You can become a policeman, a soldier, a medic, a newscaster, an investigative agent, anything. You can take roles from as little to small time crime member, or as big as a mafia boss.

Equity Gaming makes you experience things you wouldn't be able to experience in real life, in the game, through your own actions, making it an immersive experience.
